Lasting. Secure.

CertChain is a platform enabling the creation and distribution of certificates as NFTs on the Solana block-chain. From awards, to degrees, to certifications, everything is unique, verifiable, and permanent.  Long after this website may lose support, your certificates will permanently exist, enforced by the Solana block-chain and IPFS.

About

Verify

Verify your insitution with CertChain.  While the contract and tokens may be decentralized, CertChain.com acts as a buffer of credibility as a platform to create and distribute degrees and certificates as NFTs.

Create

Upload a certificate or degree by connecting your verified Solana web-wallet and automatically generate an NFT.  This NFT can then be given limited co-ownership to the recipient, and it will automatically show up in their portfolio

Connect

Link your portfolio to your electronic resume, LinkedIn, or just about anywhere! Blockchain enforced certificates make it easier to share and connect than ever before, with airtight security.
